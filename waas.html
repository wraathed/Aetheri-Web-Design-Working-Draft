Website Ownership & Termination

Clients have exclusive use of the custom-built website during the active subscription. Upon completion of the 6-month minimum contract, the service can be terminated with a 30-day written notice. After termination, the website design and code remain our intellectual property. However, if you wish to acquire the site files for independent hosting, there is a one-time buyout fee, which grants you full ownership of the site and all of its code.
